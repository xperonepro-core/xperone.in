Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

1. Digital License Nature

XperOne provides digital licenses and infrastructure access. Upon issuance of license credentials or DigitalVault access, services are considered delivered.

2. Non-Refundable License Fees

License fees are non-refundable once:

  • Access credentials are generated
  • Documentation is issued
  • Automated delivery is activated

3. Duplicate Payments

Refunds may be issued in cases of verified duplicate payments.

4. Technical Failure

If a verified technical failure prevents access, we will attempt resolution before considering refund.

5. Revenue Share Transactions

Revenue share deductions represent infrastructure processing fees and are not refundable once a transaction is completed.

6. Chargebacks

Fraudulent or unjustified chargebacks may result in suspension or permanent revocation of reseller access.

7. Contact

For refund requests, contact: support@xperone.in
